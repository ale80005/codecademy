Interior design is and amazing resource that allows any person to create a beautiful home. Although designing different rooms can seem simple, making a room have a consistent flow and energy takes time and effort. This website is designed to help people remodle rooms in their homes and have their new spaces express their personal asthetic.

* * *

Basics

  • When creating any room, no matter what style, it is important to chose inspirational photos to give yourself ideas of how you want the final product to look like.
  • Now that you have established the style of room you are going for, choose a color scheme to match your style. Decide upon a variety of colors that go well together, and chose light colors to go on the walls to make the room look and feel more airy. If you are wanting to use a darker color paint, accent walls look great on the wall where the bed is goung to be paced against.
  • After you know what style and color scheme you're aiming for, shopping will be the next task. When looking for furniature and decor items keep an open mind and look at a wide vareity of options.
  • Before buying any pieces of furniture measure your room and put in the measurments of the furniture pieces you like to make sure they all fit in the room nicely. Also before purchasing anything make sure the item you are buying matches the color scheme and asthetic of the room.

* * *

Bohemian

  • When creating a bohemian room a neutral color scheme with toned down colors is important.
  • Adding natural elements to a room like raw wood or macrame can add a very bohemian touch.
  • Incorporating tribal and medallion patterns in throw blankets, bedding, or tapetries is also key to a bohemian look.
  • Adding accesories with tassles and incorporating fairy lights and candles also add to the bohemian asthetic.

* * *

Modern/Simplistic

  • Choosing and extremely neautral and toned down color scheme is extremely important to make a room look sleek. Choose only a few colors and repeat colors througjout th room to keep a flow going.
  • In terms of furniature and accents, choosing things with clean lines and minimalistic features is key. For a modern look geometric accesories can add angles to make the room look more sharp.
  • To add interest in such a toned down style, choose furniature, accesories, and decor with differnt textures.

* * *

Whimsicle

  • When choosing a color scheme for a whimsicle look choose neautral colors with a pop of pastels.
  • Adding floral touches, soft textures, throw pillows, blackets, teepee's, and fairy lights all give the room and extra whimsicle feel.

* * *

Colorful

  • When choosing a color scheme for a colorful room, incorporating colors that work really well together and adding neutral touches to add is key for balance and flow;
  • Bright Color Scheme: Add colorful furniture mixed in with some neutral furniture, and match it with colorful throw pillows, blankets,rugs, and accesories.
  • Neutral Color Scheme with Pops of Color: Choose neautral furniture and add color in through rugs, pillows, throw blankets, and decorativ items.
  • No matter how much color you want to incorporate into your room, adding plants to add natural pops of color can really help make the room come together by adding a freshness that only plants can give.
